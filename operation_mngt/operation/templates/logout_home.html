Normal checkup

Is a diagnostic examination of the entire body systems, including: examination of the heart, kidneys, liver, lungs, and all bones of the body,it should be done twice a month.

Blood Test

The aim of the blood test is to measure the levels of the various substances present in it, such as the number of blood cells, the concentration of salts, and the levels of other elements that would indicate the presence of pathological conditions.

Body temperature

The average body temperature is 98.6 F (37 C). But the normal body temperature may range from 97 F (36.1 C) to 99 F (37.2 C) or more.
